Behavioral Analytics

What is a Heatmap?

A visual representation of where users click, move, and scroll on your site. We use heatmaps to identify precisely where you are losing customers.

Click Maps

Shows exactly which buttons or links are getting interaction. If a primary CTA isn't glowing red, the design needs to be changed.

Scroll Maps

Reveals how far down the page users are willing to scroll. This dictates where we place crucial trust markers and final conversion forms.

Why We Need It
