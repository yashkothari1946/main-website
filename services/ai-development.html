Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about implementing AI in your business.

Can you build a custom ChatGPT for my business?
Yes. We develop private AI assistants that can securely access your business data, internal documents, knowledge bases, policies, FAQs, and product information to provide accurate responses tailored to your organization.
What is RAG and how can it help my business?
RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) allows AI systems to retrieve information from your business documents, knowledge base, manuals, and internal resources before generating responses. This improves accuracy while keeping answers relevant to your organization's data.
Can AI access our internal documents and knowledge base?
Yes. We can develop secure AI systems that interact with internal documents, SOPs, policies, training materials, product manuals, customer support documentation, and company knowledge repositories.
Do you develop WhatsApp AI chatbots?
Yes. We build WhatsApp AI chatbots that can answer customer questions, qualify leads, schedule appointments, provide support, automate conversations, and integrate with business systems.
Can you create AI chatbots for websites?
Yes. We develop website chatbots and AI assistants that help businesses provide instant customer support, generate leads, answer FAQs, and improve website engagement.
Can AI automate customer support?
Yes. AI can automate common support requests, answer frequently asked questions, provide product information, assist with troubleshooting, and escalate complex issues to human teams when needed.
