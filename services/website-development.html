Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries regarding our website development and engineering process.

What types of websites do you develop?
We develop custom business websites, e-commerce websites, service-based websites, educational platforms, multi-vendor marketplaces, landing pages, custom web applications, client portals, booking systems, and enterprise solutions.
Do you build custom websites or use templates?
We offer both approaches depending on project requirements. For businesses that need unique functionality, scalability, and brand differentiation, we recommend custom website development tailored to specific goals and workflows.
Will my website be optimized for SEO?
Yes. We build websites with SEO best practices in mind, including technical optimization, page speed improvements, responsive design, schema implementation, clean URL structures, and search engine-friendly architecture.
Do you build e-commerce websites?
Yes. We develop e-commerce websites for businesses selling products online, including Shopify stores, WooCommerce websites, custom e-commerce solutions, product catalog systems, and online payment integrations.
Can you develop custom web applications?
Yes. We build custom web applications such as CRM systems, ERP platforms, admin dashboards, booking systems, customer portals, workflow management tools, and business automation solutions.
Do you provide hosting and domain support?
Yes. We can assist with hosting setup, hosting management, domain registration, DNS configuration, SSL installation, website migrations, and ongoing technical support.
Do you provide website maintenance after launch?
Yes. We offer ongoing website maintenance services that include updates, security monitoring, performance optimization, backups, bug fixes, and technical support.
Can you migrate my website from another platform?
Yes. We provide website migration services for businesses moving between hosting providers, CMS platforms, e-commerce platforms, or custom environments while minimizing downtime and preserving functionality.
