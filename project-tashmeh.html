Industry: Operations

Tech: Cloud & IoT

The Challenge

Tashmeh Industries, a legacy heavy-machinery operator, was facing severe competitive pressure due to outdated analog processes. Equipment maintenance was purely reactive, leading to costly unexpected downtime, and field operators were relying on paper-based reporting that delayed critical business decisions by weeks.

Our Solution

Vybtek deployed a comprehensive digital transformation strategy. We introduced IoT sensors to all critical machinery, feeding telemetry data into a centralized, secure cloud dashboard. We then developed a fleet of robust mobile applications for field workers to digitize their reporting workflows.

  • Predictive Maintenance: Machine learning algorithms that predict equipment failures weeks before they happen.
  • Digitized Field Ops: Custom tablet applications that sync data directly to HQ, even in offline environments.
  • Cloud Migration: Moved all legacy on-premise servers to a scalable AWS infrastructure.

The Result

By digitizing their core operations, Tashmeh Industries completely eliminated paper trails and reduced unexpected machinery downtime to near-zero. The digital overhaul positioned them as a modern, data-driven leader in an otherwise traditional industry.
