The Challenge

Catalyst Financial managed millions in institutional capital, but their core workflows were heavily reliant on physical paper trails, disjointed excel sheets, and legacy on-premise servers. They needed an absolute modernization of their operations without compromising on strict financial compliance and security.

Digitizing Workflows

Translating highly complex, heavily regulated paper-based financial processes into seamless digital interfaces.

Institutional Security

Ensuring the new cloud architecture exceeded all SOC2 and financial regulatory compliance standards.

Our Solution

Complete Digital Transformation

We architected a bespoke, highly secure cloud ecosystem that revolutionized how they manage capital.

Secure Cloud Infrastructure

Migrated their entire dataset and daily operations to a robust, compliant cloud architecture on Microsoft Azure. This allowed analysts to access critical data remotely while maintaining enterprise-grade security protocols.

Custom Client Portals

Developed proprietary web applications that completely eliminated paper trails. Investors now use a sleek, intuitive digital dashboard to track performance, sign documents digitally, and manage their portfolios in real-time.
